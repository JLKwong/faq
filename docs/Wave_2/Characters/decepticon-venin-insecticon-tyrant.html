TFTCG FAQ
Wave 2 Characters

Decepticon Venin - Insecticon Tyrant

  1. What happens if this is KO’d and my opponent has fewer than 2 cards in hand?
    If they have 1 card in hand, that card is scrapped. If they have an empty hand, nothing happens.
  2. What happens if Venin and another of my Insecticons are KO’d at the same time?
    In that case, Venin’s ability will trigger twice. Your opponent will scrap 4 cards from their hand.
