TFTCG FAQ
Wave 2 Characters

Razorclaw - Predacon Leader

  1. When I flip Razorclaw to bot mode, can he still attack tapped characters?
    Yes.
  2. Can Razorclaw attack an untapped character that has stealth if there are other characters he could attack?
    Yes. Abilities like stealth and brave still function. You just treat untapped characters as though they were tapped when determining what Razorclaw can attack.
