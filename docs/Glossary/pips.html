Pips

  1. What is 'Black Pip'?
    A black oblong in the right hand corner of a battle card. When attacking, the number of black pips on the battle cards the attacker flips are added to the overall
  2. What is 'Blue Pip'?
    A blue oblong in the right hand corner of a battle card. When defending, the number of blue pips on the battle cards the defender flips are added to the overall defence.
  3. What is 'Green Pip'?
    A green oblong in the right hand corner of a battle card. When battle cards are flipped in either defence or attack, one battle card with a green pip on it may be swapped with a battle card in the player's hand.
  4. What is 'Orange Pip'?
    An orange oblong in the right hand corner of a battle card. When attacking, the number of orange pips on the battle cards the attacker flips are added to the overall attack.
  5. What is 'White Pip'?
    An white oblong in the right hand corner of a battle card. When attacking or defending, the first white pip flipped means the player flips two more battle cards. Additional white pips do not lead to more battle cards being flipped unless an effect explicitly states it does.
