TFTCG FAQ
Wave 1 Characters

Nemesis Prime, Dark Clone

  1. Can I look at the bottom card of my deck before putting it under Nemesis Prime?
    No. Neither you nor your opponent will know what card it is.
  2. What happens to the cards under Nemesis Prime when he’s KO’d?
    They’re put into the scrap pile, along with any Upgrades he had.
