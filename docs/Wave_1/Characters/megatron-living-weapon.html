TFTCG FAQ
Wave 1 Characters

Megatron, Living Weapon

  1. Does Megatron’s ability mean he can hold three Weapons?
    Yes. He can have a Weapon in his Weapon slot as well as Weapons in his Armor and/or Utility slots.
  2. If I play a Weapon on Megatron, do I have to choose which slot it’s going in?
    No. As long as Megatron can hold all the Upgrades attached to him, you don’t have to specify which slot anything is in. For example, if Megatron has two Weapons and a Utility, and you play an Armor onto him, you can choose at that time which Weapon it will replace.
  3. When Megatron flips to alt mode, can he keep all his Weapons?
    Yes. The bot mode ability applies only to how the Weapons are put on Megatron.
