TFTCG FAQ
Wave 1 Characters

Optimus Prime, Battlefield Legend

  1. If I flip a white battle icon, do I flip the extra cards before using Optimus Prime’s ability?
    Yes. The ability works after you’ve flipped all cards, including extra cards from abilities like Bold or from white battle icons.
  2. If I play one of the Actions I flip, do I still count its orange battle icons?
    Yes. The Action card was still flipped for the attack, so its orange battle icons will still count.
  3. If the Action I play gives Optimus Prime Bold, can I flip more battle cards?
    No. You’ve already flipped cards for that attack.
