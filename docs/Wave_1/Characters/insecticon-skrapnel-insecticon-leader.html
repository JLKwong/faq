TFTCG FAQ
Wave 1 Characters

Insecticon Skrapnel, Insecticon leader

  1. If the attacking character would deal more than 3 damage, what happens to the extra damage?
    The extra damage doesn’t happen. The attacking character will deal only 3 damage.
  2. Let’s say Insecticon Skrapnel defends and the attacker has an ability that deals damage to him. Does that damage count for Insecticon Skrapnel’s ability?
    Yes. Any damage dealt to Insecticon Skrapnel while he’s defending will count. If the attacker has an ability that deals 1 damage to Insecticon Skrpanel, then he can’t take more than 2 more damage from the attack.
